A Regenerative Education Institution in Bhutan

Education Rooted in Nature, Culture, and Purpose

Jamyang Ecoversity is building a hands-on school for regenerative living — where learners grow food, build with natural materials, and develop the skills and clarity to live meaningful lives.

Learn About Us Support the Mission

A Different Kind of Learning

  • Learn by doing — grow food, build with natural materials, practice traditional crafts
  • Rooted in place — connected to land, culture, and community in Bhutan
  • Holistic growth — ecological care, practical skills, and personal development in one experience
  • Designed to adapt — a model that can be replicated in communities anywhere

Why This Matters

Too much of modern education leads to disconnection — from the land, from community, from purpose. We are building something different.

Learning Disconnected from Life

Most education happens in classrooms, far from the land, skills, and relationships that matter most.

Youth Seeking Purpose

Young people graduate without clarity, practical skills, or pathways to work that aligns with their values.

Wisdom Fading

Traditional knowledge and place-based intelligence are disappearing with each generation.

What We Focus On

Four interconnected areas that form the heart of our emerging curriculum.

Regenerative Agriculture

Growing food in ways that heal ecosystems, build soil, and create resilient local food systems.

Natural Building

Creating functional, beautiful structures using local materials and ecological design principles.

Cultural Wisdom

Preserving and practicing ancestral knowledge, traditional crafts, and intergenerational learning.

Holistic Wellbeing

Nurturing inner growth, mindfulness, and the physical, emotional, and spiritual health of every learner.
