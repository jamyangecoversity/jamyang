Learning & Programs

Eight integrated learning areas and hands-on programs designed for regenerative living, meaningful livelihood, and holistic human development.

Learning by Doing, Living by Learning

Our curriculum weaves together eight essential learning areas into immersive, hands-on programs. Rather than isolated subjects, these domains interconnect — preparing learners to think and act holistically across cultures, landscapes, and communities. Whether you seek a comprehensive immersion or focused skill-building, we offer pathways that guide you toward a regenerative future.

01

Regenerative Agriculture and Food Systems

Learn to cultivate food and fiber in ways that heal ecosystems, build soil health, and create resilient local food systems.

Soil Health & Composting Seed Saving & Biodiversity Agroecology Permaculture Design Water Stewardship Food Forests Kitchen Gardening Small Livestock Integration Climate-Resilient Farming Local Food Systems
02

Natural Building and Ecological Design

Master the art and science of creating beautiful, functional buildings using local, natural materials and ecological principles.

Vernacular Architecture Earth-Based Construction Bamboo & Timber Systems Passive Solar Design Natural Plasters & Finishes Ecological Sanitation Rainwater Harvesting Site Planning
03

Traditional Knowledge and Cultural Literacy

Connect with ancestral wisdom and cultural practices that sustain communities and ecological relationships.

Values & Worldview Indigenous Ecological Knowledge Crafts & Artisanal Skills Intergenerational Learning Oral Traditions Land-Based Practices Identity & Rootedness
04

Holistic Wellbeing

Cultivate physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual health through integrated practices and community care.

Mental Wellbeing Mindfulness & Presence Physical Health & Movement Nutrition & Food Wisdom Community Care Healthy Relationships Responsible Living

Learning That Reconnects Head, Heart, and Hands

Every domain is experienced through direct practice, mentorship, and real-world application — never in isolation.

05

Regenerative Livelihoods and Social Enterprise

Create meaningful work that generates value for people and planet while providing dignified livelihoods.

Livelihood Design Local Enterprise Development Ethical Entrepreneurship Cooperative Models Value-Added Products Craft-Based Income Financial Literacy Business Planning
06

Ecological Stewardship and Restoration

Develop the knowledge and skills to protect, restore, and enhance natural systems and biodiversity.

Watershed Awareness Biodiversity Conservation Afforestation & Reforestation Land Restoration Waste Reduction Circular Systems Ecosystem Monitoring
07

Appropriate Technology and Innovation

Learn to select, create, and apply technologies that serve human needs while respecting ecological limits.

Life-Serving Tools Renewable Energy Basics Digital Literacy Responsible Technology Farming Tools Building Technologies Communication Systems
08

Leadership, Purpose, and Community Engagement

Develop the capacity to lead, organize, and mobilize communities for positive change and regenerative action.

Self-Awareness Values-Based Leadership Communication & Facilitation Community Organizing Action Research Service Learning Project Development
Flagship Program

Foundational Certificate Program

A comprehensive immersion in regenerative living and learning

Duration

3-6 months immersive learning experience

Format

Residential, hands-on, community-based learning

Who It's For

Youth and adults seeking meaningful livelihood pathways

Focus Areas

Practical regenerative living skills integrated with personal development

What You'll Learn

Our foundational program provides comprehensive training in regenerative agriculture, natural building, traditional knowledge, holistic wellbeing, and community leadership. Through daily practice, project-based learning, and mentorship, you'll develop the skills, confidence, and clarity needed to create meaningful work that serves people and planet.

Key Outcomes

  • Practical skills in multiple regenerative domains
  • Deep understanding of ecological principles
  • Cultural literacy and traditional knowledge appreciation
  • Leadership and community organizing abilities
  • Entrepreneurial mindset and business planning skills
  • Personal wellbeing and resilience practices

Apprenticeships & Mentorships

Learn directly from experienced practitioners in real-world settings

Farming Apprenticeship

Work alongside experienced regenerative farmers in all aspects of seasonal growing, from soil preparation to harvest and market.

  • 6-12 month commitment
  • Stipend and housing provided
  • Weekly learning sessions
  • Independent project opportunities

Natural Building Apprenticeship

Learn construction skills through hands-on building projects using natural materials and ecological design principles.

  • 3-9 month commitment
  • Tool training and safety
  • Design and planning experience
  • Portfolio development

Crafts & Traditional Skills Mentorship

Work with master artisans to learn traditional crafts, from weaving and pottery to woodworking and metalwork.

  • Flexible duration
  • One-on-one mentorship
  • Business and marketing guidance
  • Community showcase opportunities

Social Enterprise Mentorship

Develop and launch your regenerative business idea with guidance from experienced social entrepreneurs.

  • 6 month program
  • Business planning support
  • Networking opportunities
  • Potential seed funding access
