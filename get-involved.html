Get Involved

Jamyang Ecoversity is in its early stages — and there are meaningful ways to be part of it right now.

Building This Together

Creating a new model of education takes more than a vision — it takes people. Whether you want to learn, contribute resources, share expertise, or help spread the word, your involvement makes a real difference. Here are the most direct ways to participate.

How You Can Participate

Choose the path that fits you best.

01

Apply as a Learner

If you are a young person or adult looking for a different kind of education — one rooted in nature, hands-on practice, and community — we want to hear from you. Our foundational program is currently being developed, and we are building a list of interested learners for our first cohort.

Express Interest
02

Support the Mission

Your financial support helps us build the campus, develop programs, and make education accessible. Whether it is a one-time gift, ongoing support, or in-kind contribution — every bit helps this vision take root.

Make a Contribution
03

Partner With Us

We welcome partnerships with educational institutions, NGOs, community organizations, and government bodies who share our values. Together we can co-develop programs, exchange knowledge, and amplify impact.

Explore Partnership
04

Volunteer or Share Expertise

Have skills in farming, building, education, communications, or organizational development? We are looking for people willing to contribute their time and knowledge — on-site or remotely.

Offer Your Skills

Every Contribution Creates Ripples

Your involvement helps build an educational model that can transform lives and landscapes — in Bhutan and beyond.

What Your Support Makes Possible

Here is where resources are going right now as we build from the ground up.

Campus Development

Building the physical infrastructure — gardens, natural buildings, workshop spaces, and living quarters — that will become the learning environment.

Program Design

Developing the curriculum, learning materials, and partnerships needed to launch our first programs with depth and quality.

Learner Access

Creating scholarships and support systems so that education at Jamyang Ecoversity is accessible regardless of financial background.
