Frequently Asked Questions

How can I apply to your programs?

Visit our Programs page or contact us directly for application details. We offer rolling admissions for most programs.

Do you offer financial aid or scholarships?

Yes. We offer need-based scholarships and fellowships. Visit our Get Involved page for details on sponsoring a learner or applying for support.

Can I visit the campus?

Campus visits are available by appointment. Please contact us at least two weeks in advance to schedule a tour.

How can I partner with Jamyang Ecoversity?

We welcome partnerships with educational institutions, community organizations, businesses, and government agencies worldwide. Reach out with your ideas.

Do you offer online or distance learning?

Our core programs are residential and hands-on. We are developing digital resources and remote workshops. Join our newsletter for updates.

How can I volunteer or contribute expertise?

We welcome volunteers and practitioners from around the world. Visit our Get Involved page or contact us with your skills and availability.
