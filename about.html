About Jamyang Ecoversity

An emerging living-learning center in Bhutan, reconnecting education with nature, culture, and purpose.

Where We Come From

Jamyang Ecoversity grew from a simple question: what would education look like if it taught us how to live well — not just how to earn a living?

  • The need — ecological crisis and growing disconnection demand a different kind of learning
  • The response — education rooted in land, shaped by tradition, and oriented toward regeneration
  • The place — we are building this vision in Bhutan, where practice, wisdom, and inner growth come together

What We Believe

Education Should Be Lived

The deepest learning happens through doing — in the garden, the workshop, and the community, not only in the classroom.

Wisdom Lives in Place

Real knowledge is connected to land, culture, and community. When we learn from where we are, we learn who we are.

Success Is More Than a Career

A good education prepares people for meaningful lives — not just jobs, but purpose, health, relationships, and contribution.

Traditional Knowledge Matters

Ancestral practices and indigenous wisdom hold vital answers for the challenges we face today and tomorrow.

What We Are Building

  • Hands-on learning — grow food, build with natural materials, practice traditional crafts
  • Inner development — cultivate clarity, purpose, and personal wellbeing
  • Integrated approach — ecology, culture, skills, and personal growth woven into one experience
  • Adaptable model — rooted in Bhutan, designed to work anywhere

Our Vision

A world where education nurtures grounded, capable, and community-centered people who lead the transition to regenerative ways of living.

Our Mission

To create a living-learning space where people develop the skills, values, and purpose to live meaningfully and contribute to a thriving future for all life.

What Guides Us

Regeneration

We heal and restore ecosystems, communities, and relationships through everything we learn and practice.

Rootedness

We connect deeply to place, culture, and community while remaining open to wisdom from all traditions.

Learning by Doing

We ground all knowledge in hands-on experience — in the field, the workshop, and the kitchen.

Community

We learn together, support one another, and take collective responsibility for our shared future.

Stewardship

We care for the land, water, air, and all living systems that sustain life on Earth.

Wholeness

We nurture the physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual health of every person who walks through our doors.
