About Jamyang Ecoversity

A living-learning center for regenerative education, rooted in land, culture, community, and purpose.

Our Story

Jamyang Ecoversity was born from a simple conviction: education should teach us how to live well, care for the land, strengthen community, and create work that matters. In a time of ecological crisis and social fragmentation, we saw the urgent need for a different kind of learning — one that reconnects people with the living world.

We started as a conversation among educators, farmers, builders, and community leaders who shared a belief that the most powerful learning happens through direct experience. Together, we set out to create a place where head, heart, and hands work as one — where knowledge is rooted in place, shaped by tradition, and oriented toward regeneration.

The Challenge We Address

Abstraction Over Experience

Mainstream education prioritizes theory and testing over genuine understanding, hands-on skill, and practical capability.

Disconnected from Place

Learning is confined to classrooms, severed from the land, ecology, and community contexts where knowledge truly comes alive.

Narrow Definition of Success

Education is reduced to employment preparation, overlooking the development of whole, rooted, purpose-driven human beings.

Erasure of Local Wisdom

Standardized curricula worldwide displace indigenous knowledge, local culture, and the place-based intelligence communities need most.

Our Response

Jamyang Ecoversity offers a hands-on, place-based, values-rooted, and globally adaptable model of education. We believe learning happens best through doing, that knowledge should be connected to place, and that education should serve both individual growth and collective wellbeing.

Our approach weaves traditional wisdom with contemporary innovation, creating learning experiences that are both timeless and timely. We cultivate not just skilled practitioners, but thoughtful human beings who understand their role in building regenerative futures — wherever they are in the world.

Our Vision

A world where education nurtures grounded, capable, creative, and community-centered people who lead the transition to regenerative ways of living — wherever they call home.

Our Mission

To create a transformative learning space where people unlearn extractive models of success, relearn regenerative ways of living, and develop the skills, values, and purpose to contribute meaningfully to a thriving future for all life.

Our Values

Regeneration

We heal and restore ecosystems, communities, and relationships through everything we learn and practice.

Dignity

We honor the worth of every person, every form of knowledge, and every living being.

Rootedness

We connect deeply to place, culture, and community while remaining open to wisdom from all traditions.

Wisdom

We integrate ancestral knowledge with appropriate innovation, honoring both ancient and emerging insights.

Community

We learn together, support one another, and take collective responsibility for our shared future.

Stewardship

We care for the land, water, air, and all living systems that sustain life on Earth.

Resilience

We cultivate adaptability, creativity, and inner strength in the face of change and uncertainty.

Wellbeing

We nurture the physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual health of individuals and communities.

Practical Learning

We learn by doing — grounding all knowledge in hands-on experience and real-world application.

Ethical Innovation

We develop and apply tools and systems that serve life and respect the boundaries of the living world.
